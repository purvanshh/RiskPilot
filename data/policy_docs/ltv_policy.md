# Loan-to-Value (LTV) Policy

## 1. Maximum LTV Thresholds
- **Primary Limit**: The maximum allowable Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio for standard mortgage/property-backed loans is **80%** (0.80).
- **Exceptions**: LTV ratios up to **85%** (0.85) may be approved only if the applicant's credit score is greater than 720 and their DTI is under 35%.
- **Hard Reject**: Any LTV exceeding **85%** (0.85) is a hard policy violation and must be flagged for denial.
