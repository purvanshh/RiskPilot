# Income Verification Policy

## 1. Documentation Requirements
- To verify income, the applicant must provide at least **two** of the following documents:
  1. A recent Bank Statement showing monthly payroll deposits.
  2. A recent Pay Slip reflecting gross income and year-to-date earnings.
  3. An official Employment Letter confirming current salary and tenure.
- The KYC agent must verify that the income reported on the application aligns (within 10%) with the deposits shown in the bank statements and pay slips.
- Any discrepancy exceeding 10% must be flagged for manual review.
