# Debt-to-Income (DTI) Policy

## 1. Maximum DTI Thresholds
- **Standard Maximum DTI**: The maximum debt-to-income ratio allowed for standard approvals is **45%** (0.45).
- **Hard Cap DTI**: Under no circumstances shall an applicant be approved with a DTI exceeding **50%** (0.50). Any DTI above 50% constitutes a policy violation and should result in a denial recommendations by default.
- **Borderline DTI**: DTI ratios between 40% and 45% are considered borderline and should prompt the Arbitrator agent to flag for manual review.
