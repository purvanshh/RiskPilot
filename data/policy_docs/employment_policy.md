# Employment Stability Policy

## 1. Employment Tenure Requirements
- **Minimum Stability**: Applicants must have a minimum of **12 months** of continuous employment history with their current employer, or within the same line of work.
- **Exceptions**: Less than 12 months (e.g., 6-11 months) may be accepted if the applicant was in a similar role previously with no employment gaps, subject to manual review.
- **Direct Denial**: Applicants with less than 6 months of employment history are not eligible for approval.
